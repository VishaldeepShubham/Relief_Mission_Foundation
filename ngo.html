Relief Mission Foundation is a registered charitable foundation. Our aim is creating a long and strong relationship between individuals to generate funds for the well-being of children and needy persons.

Situated in Jaipur, Relief Mission Foundation works closely with children taking care of their day-to-day needs. With your help and support, we can change the way these children live and provide them a new meaning to their lives.

Relief Mission Foundation needs your support to help children and women.

Our Missions

Medical Emergency

"I won't give up on you, so don't give up on me." We provide financial assistance to sick children so that they can be properly treated. We work 24√ó7 to ensure robust health with nutritional food.

Women Empowerment

Women Empowerment refers to giving power to women - providing opportunities to flourish financially, academically, and socially. We work towards equal opportunities and education for all women.

Better Quality of Education

Education is the right of every child. We help children get better access to primary and secondary education. Your support opens new doors of hope and lights the lamp in their darkness.

Shelter for Homeless

"Homelessness is not a choice, but rather a journey that many find themselves in." We provide shelter and good livelihood to children who struggle on the roadways of our country.

Plant a Tree, Grant Life

"If you plant a tree, you plant so many lives." Trees make the environment cleaner and provide oxygen. They're essential for life to flourish and help reduce pollution effects.

Animals - The Beauty of Nature

We're actively involved in animal welfare. Our goal is to give the gift of life to as many street animals as possible and enrich the lives of those who adopt them.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will my small contribution make a difference? +
Every drop in the ocean counts. No matter how small you think your contribution may be, we assure you that it'll make a big difference to children's lives.
What is the advantage of becoming a monthly donor? +
When you donate on a monthly basis, not only do you help us create a larger impact on the lives of children, but you also help us plan our work at the grassroots level more efficiently. Auto-recurring monthly donations help reduce costs.
Are my online transactions secure? +
Absolutely. We have stringent security measures in place to ensure that critically sensitive information, such as your personal information and credit/debit card details, are protected.
